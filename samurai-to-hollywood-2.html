The Force Is Bushidō
Japanese Samurai Cinema
⚔️
The Ronin — Masterless Warrior
A disbanded order, masters dead, serving a code rather than a state. The skill demands a spiritual discipline, not just muscle.
🗡️
The Katana as Identity
The sword is inseparable from the warrior's soul. To wield it demands years of inner cultivation, not just technique.
🎋
Bushidō — The Way of the Warrior
A moral code: loyalty, honor, self-sacrifice. The warrior serves values larger than himself, even when no master commands it.
🎬
The Hidden Fortress (1958)
George Lucas's direct source for Star Wars — the perspective device, the princess, the general who guides her across enemy lines.
"I was inspired by Kurosawa's use of multiple characters to tell the story of a battle. The Hidden Fortress really inspired Star Wars."
