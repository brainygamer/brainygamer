1. Culinary Highlights

Street Food Excellence

Trinidad Ritual de Sabores

Ignacio Allende s/n-Local 123-125, Coyoacán Market

A masterclass in modern Mexican street food nestled within the vibrant Mercado de Coyoacán. Internationally recognized for its sophisticated vegan options alongside famous meat selections.

Hours Tue – Sun: 10:30 AM – 6:00 PM (Closed Monday)
Sundays can see waits of 15+ minutes. Arrive by 1:00 PM for the best selection.
🎷

2. Jazz & Nightlife

World-Class Acoustics
Premier Venue

Zinco Jazz Club

Motolinia 20, Centro Histórico

Tucked away in the basement of a former bank vault — an authentic 20th-century speakeasy with velvet curtains and world-class acoustics.

Reservation WhatsApp: +52 55 3936 9590 (highly recommended)
Walk-ins Only after 10:00 PM if space permits
Cover ~MX$150–$300 (often cash only at door)
Doors Usually 9:00 PM — check @zincojazzclub on Instagram
Reserve via WhatsApp at least 48 hours in advance for weekend sets. Card accepted for food & beverages.

A hidden gem close to home — the Cineteca's outdoor terrace blends spectacular architecture with community-driven live music.

When Fridays & Saturdays starting at 7:00 PM
Cost Free — open to the public
Atmosphere Intimate lighting, craft beers & cocktails on-site
