World Cinema · Character Guide

Superboys of Malegaon

Dir. Reema Kagti  ·  2024  ·  Hindi

Director
Nasir Shaikh
Adarsh Gourav

The visionary at the center of it all. Nasir runs his family's video parlor and becomes obsessed with making films for Malegaon's own audience. He's the driving force behind every production — and his ambitions grow almost faster than his friendships can hold.

The Writer
Farogh
Vineet Kumar Singh

Nasir's poet-screenwriter and intellectual foil. Farogh brings literary ambition to the group's scrappy productions. He eventually leaves for Mumbai to chase a career in Bollywood — where he quickly discovers that the industry has no interest in stories from Malegaon.

The Soul
Shafique
Shashank Arora

Nasir's most loyal friend and the group's lead actor — though he rarely gets the recognition he deserves. Quiet, selfless, and deeply devoted, Shafique dreams of being seen as a hero. His arc becomes the film's most emotional thread, especially as his health declines.

The Actor
Akram
Anuj Singh Duhan

One of the core "superboys" of the troupe, Akram takes on acting roles in Nasir's productions. Like others in the group, he struggles with being underpaid and uncredited, which eventually strains the friendship and the collaboration.

The Crew
Irfan
Saqib Ayub

Another member of the founding circle, Irfan helps bring Nasir's films to life. His presence underlines the film's central theme: that moviemaking in Malegaon is a collective act, built on trust and shared passion rather than contracts or credits.

The Anchor
Shabeena
Muskkaan Jaferi

Nasir's wife, and one of the film's most grounded presences. Shabeena understands that cinema is Nasir's "first love" — and loves him anyway. Her relationship with Nasir is portrayed with rare emotional maturity: quiet, honest, and free of melodrama.
