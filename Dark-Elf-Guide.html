The Dunmer Sorcerer

Ebonheart Pact & Faction Origin Guide

🔥 Immediate Setup (Skip Tutorial)

1

Spawn & Ignore: After skipping the tutorial, you'll spawn in the latest DLC zone. Ignore the local quest-givers and find the nearest Wayshrine.

2

Teleport to Stonefalls: Open your map and look for Davon's Watch in the Stonefalls region. Teleport there immediately.

3

Trigger the Main Story: As soon as you step off the Wayshrine, a "Hooded Figure" will approach you. Talk to her to start the "Soul Shriven in Coldharbour" quest. This is the Harborage storyline.

4

Backtrack for Faction Context: Go to the docks in Davon's Watch and find the Boatswain to go to Bleakrock Isle. This is the true beginning of the Ebonheart Pact story.

Chronological Questing Order

The Faction Story

  • Bleakrock Isle (Starter Island)
  • Bal Foyen (Prologue)
  • Stonefalls (Dunmer Heartlands)
  • Deshaan (Mournhold City)
  • Shadowfen (Argonian Marshes)

The Harborage (Main Story)

Return to the Harborage in Stonefalls whenever the Prophet calls.

  • Soul Shriven in Coldharbour
  • Chasing Shadows
  • Castle of the Worm
  • Halls of Torment
  • God of Schemes (Finale)

*Note: You can swap between these two paths at your own pace. Neither will penalize the other.

Sorcerer "Powerful Mage" Build

Attributes: Magicka Is Everything

Put 64 points into Magicka. Do not put points into Health or Stamina. As a Dark Elf Sorcerer, your Magicka pool directly determines your damage output and shield strength.
